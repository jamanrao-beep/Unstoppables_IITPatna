## ğŸ›‘ The Invisible Crisis

Current environmental monitoring relies on massive, city-wide averages that mask the toxic reality of localized industrial and traffic zones. A sensor in a green park cannot warn a commuter about hazardous PM2.5 smog just 2km away.

**Pro Atmos Guard** eliminates these spatial blind spots. It is a decentralized, machine-learning-powered environmental command center that predicts pollution spikes before they happen and routes commuters safely around them.
